Create a full-screen, no-scroll, step-based 3D website for a college fest named “VYOMANG”.

STYLE & THEME:
- Dark black background with gold accents
- Premium, royal, futuristic feel
- Subtle 3D animated abstract shapes and particles in the background
- Smooth camera transitions between screens
- App-like experience (screens instead of scrolling)
- Use glassmorphism panels for forms
- Soft glow, depth, and shadows
- Optimized for both desktop and mobile

SCREEN 1 – EMAIL VERIFICATION (FIRST SCREEN):
- Full-screen 3D background
- Centered glass panel
- Text: “Verify Your Email to Continue”

- Button: “Verify OTP”
- Only after correct OTP, proceed to next screen
- Show smooth success animation before transition

SCREEN 2 – HOME / INTRO:
- 3D animated hero background
- Large title text: “VYOMANG”
- Subtitle: “Not just a fest. A legacy.”
- Button: “Buy Ticket”
- Button triggers smooth 3D transition to ticket screen

SCREEN 3 – TICKET DETAILS:
- Single highlighted 3D ticket card
- Ticket Name: “Vyomang Entry Pass”

SCREEN 4 – REGISTRATION FORM:
- Glassmorphism form panel
- Fields:
  - Full Name
  - Registration Number
  - Email (pre-filled from verification, non-editable)
  - Phone Number

SCREEN 5 – UPI PAYMENT SCREEN:

SCREEN 7 – SUCCESS & CONFIRMATION:

  “Payment Submitted Successfully”

INTERACTIONS & ANIMATION:
- No scrolling anywhere
